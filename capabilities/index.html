Engineering Capabilities

We don't just sell blades; we sell performance. Our process is driven by proprietary heat treatment technologies and Hong Kong engineering standards.

Material Selection

We apply our "4-Factor Framework" to select the perfect alloy. From standard Tool Steel to Extreme Carbide, we match the steel to your specific material hardness and abrasiveness.

Compare Blade Materials →

  • D2 / SKD11 (High Wear)

Advanced Heat Treatment

We utilize Nitrogen Atmosphere Protection to prevent oxidation. For carbide blades, we use Brazing-Quenching Composite technology to ensure an unbreakable bond.

  • Nitrogen Atmosphere Protection
  • Brazing-Quenching Composite
  • Deep Cryogenic (-196°C)

Precision Grinding

We solve the warping issue of long blades using Phase Transformation Superplasticity Straightening. This ensures your blades are perfectly straight and stress-free.

  • Superplastic Straightening
  • Mirror Surface Finish (Ra < 0.2)
  • Tolerance: ±0.005mm

OEM Contract Manufacturing

We provide private-label manufacturing for machine builders. We can laser mark your logo on the blades and use your custom packaging for blind drop-shipping.

  • Private Label Laser Marking
  • Custom Packaging
  • Blind Drop-Shipping

Inventory Management

For high-volume partners, we offer stocking programs. We keep your critical blades in our warehouse, ready to ship the same day you need them.

  • Same-Day Shipping
  • Safety Stock Programs
  • Global Logistics
