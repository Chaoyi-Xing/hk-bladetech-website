Our Capabilities

Engineering Capabilities

We don't just sell blades; we sell performance. Our manufacturing process is driven by Hong Kong engineering standards and rigorous quality control.

Reverse Engineering

No drawings? No problem. Send us your worn-out sample. We use CMM to generate precise CAD blueprints, often improving upon the original OEM design.

  • Digital Blueprint Creation

Material Selection

Selecting the right alloy is 50% of performance. We analyze your application (abrasive vs sticky) and prescribe the exact steel grade to maximize lifespan.

Compare Blade Materials →

  • D2 / SKD11 Tool Steel
  • Tungsten Carbide Inlays
  • Powder Metallurgy (PM) Steels

Advanced Heat Treatment

Hardness is nothing without toughness. We utilize vacuum heat treatment followed by Deep Cryogenic Treatment (-196°C) to significantly increase wear resistance.

  • Vacuum Hardening
  • Cryogenic Processing (-196°C)
  • Triple Tempering Cycle

Precision Grinding

Our 5-axis CNC grinding centers ensure ultra-tight tolerances. For slitter knives, we achieve a mirror-finish (Ra < 0.2) to prevent adhesive buildup.

  • Tolerance: ±0.005mm
  • Surface Finish: Ra < 0.2
  • Perfect Concentricity

OEM Contract Manufacturing

We provide private-label manufacturing for machine builders. We can laser mark your logo on the blades and use your custom packaging for blind drop-shipping.

  • Private Label Laser Marking
  • Custom Packaging
  • Blind Drop-Shipping

Inventory Management

For high-volume partners, we offer stocking programs. We keep your critical blades in our warehouse, ready to ship the same day you need them.

  • Same-Day Shipping
  • Safety Stock Programs
  • Global Logistics
