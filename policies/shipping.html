Shipping Policy

Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

1. Order Processing

In-Stock Items: Typically ship within 24-48 hours after payment confirmation.

Custom Manufacturing (OEM): Lead times vary based on complexity and material availability (typically 3-5 weeks). An estimated ship date will be provided on your Proforma Invoice.

2. Shipping Methods

We ship globally from our logistics center in Hong Kong. Available methods include:

  • Express Courier: DHL, FedEx, UPS (3-6 business days). Best for urgent or light shipments.
  • Air Freight: (5-10 days). Cost-effective for medium weight orders (>45kg).
  • Ocean Freight: (20-40 days). Best for heavy bulk orders (>500kg).

3. Incoterms & Customs

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, standard shipping terms are FOB Hong Kong or EXW (Ex Works).

Import Duties & Taxes: The buyer is responsible for all import duties, taxes, and customs clearance fees in the destination country. HK BladeTech cannot estimate these fees.

4. Inspection Upon Delivery

Please inspect your package immediately upon delivery. If the packaging appears damaged, please note this on the carrier's delivery receipt and take photos before opening. Any claims for shipping damage must be reported to the carrier and HK BladeTech within 48 hours.
