Return Policy

Return Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

At HK BladeTech, we stand behind the quality of our industrial knives. However, if you need to return a standard stock item, please review the guidelines below.

1. Return Authorization (RMA) Required

No returns will be accepted without a prior Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number. Please contact our support team at info@hk-bladetech.com to request an RMA before shipping any product back. Returns received without an RMA may be refused.

2. Eligibility for Returns

  • Requests must be made within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in 100% resalable condition. Blades with rust, mounting marks, or edge damage cannot be returned.
  • The original packing slip must be included.

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are final sale and cannot be returned:

  • Custom-made / OEM blades manufactured to your drawings.
  • Used or sharpened blades.
  • Items marked as "Clearance" or "Final Sale".

4. Shipping Costs & Restocking Fee

The customer is responsible for all return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance, as we are not responsible for lost return shipments.

A standard 15% restocking fee may apply to all non-defective returns to cover inspection and repacking costs. This fee will be deducted from your refund.

5. Defective or Incorrect Items

If you received a defective item or the wrong part, please contact us within 5 days of receipt with photos. We will arrange a replacement and cover the shipping costs for the correction.
