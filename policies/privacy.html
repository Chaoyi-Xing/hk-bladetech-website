Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

HK BladeTech respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal and proprietary information you share with us, especially technical drawings and intellectual property.

1. Information Collection

We collect information necessary to process orders and provide engineering quotes, including:

  • Contact details (Name, Email, Phone, Company Address).
  • Technical Data (CAD drawings, blueprints, material specifications).
  • Transaction history and billing information.

2. Protection of Intellectual Property (IP)

We understand that your custom blade designs may be trade secrets. HK BladeTech strictly maintains the confidentiality of all OEM drawings submitted for quotation. We do not share your technical drawings with third parties other than our internal manufacturing team.

3. Use of Information

Your data is used solely for:

  • Order fulfillment and shipping.
  • Customer service and technical support.
  • Internal analytics to improve our manufacturing processes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption and secure servers, to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. This excludes trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website or shipping your orders (e.g., DHL/FedEx), provided those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
