About Us

Manufacturer of Precision Industrial Blades

Founded in Ma'anshan, China – the heart of the machine knife industry – HK BladeTech has grown from a local workshop into a global supplier of high-performance recycling blades. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern CNC precision to deliver blades that last longer and cut better.

Our Factory & Capabilities

We are not just a trading company; we own the machines. Our facility in Ma'anshan is equipped with state-of-the-art machinery to handle every step of the blade manufacturing process.

From raw material cutting and vacuum heat treatment to precision CNC grinding and final inspection, we maintain strict control over quality.

  • Vacuum Heat Treatment: Ensures uniform hardness and structural integrity.
  • 5-Axis CNC Grinding: For complex geometries and sharp edges.
  • Strict QC Lab: Hardness testing, dimensional analysis, and flaw detection.
