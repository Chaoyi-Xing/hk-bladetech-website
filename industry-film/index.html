Flawless HK Film & Foil Converting Blades

In the converting industry, a clean cut is everything. Dust, rough edges, and tearing can ruin an entire roll of film or foil. HK BladeTech manufactures ultra-precision HK circular slitter knives, bottom bands, and straight converting blades designed for exact tolerances.

Whether you are slitting BOPP, PET, aluminum foil, or non-woven textiles, our highly polished cutting edges reduce friction and heat build-up. This guarantees flawless slit quality even at high web speeds.

We offer a variety of edge profiles and bevel angles, customized to your specific materials, providing high-quality HK aftermarket replacement knives for major slitting systems.
